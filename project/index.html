What is congenital heart disease?

Congenital heart disease (CHD) is a condition present at birth that affects how the heart forms and works. It ranges from simple issues to complex ones that may need surgery. We keep information clear, accessible, and supportive for families and caregivers..Heart Warrior Philippines is a nonprofit supporting families and communities affected by CHD. Our Mission

Heart Warriors Philippines aims to spread congenital acquired heart disease awareness and is dedicated in providing medical information, both to parents and patients. It also seeks donors and benefactors who will give financial and moral support to families living with this heart disease.

Who We Support?

  • Patients navigating life with congenital heart conditions
  • Families & caregivers seeking guidance and encouragement
  • Advocacy groups & partners working to spread awareness and improve care

We stand beside you, offering compassion, resources, and a community that understands.
