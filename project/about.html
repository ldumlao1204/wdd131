Heart Warrior Philippines

Heart Warriors Philippines was founded on May 23, 2011 by Gen Cabrera, originally under the name Congenital Heart Disease Philippines. In December 2016, the group was renamed to better reflect its mission of supporting all patients affected by serious heart conditions

Our History

Created from a parents promise to share knowledge and extend help, Heart Warriors Philippines emerged at a time when resources and support for congenital heart disease were scarce in the country. Gen Cabrera dedicated herself to building a community where families could access information, share experiences, and find guidance in navigating medical and financial challenges.

Over the years, the organization has partnered with hospitals, NGOs, and programs such as the Rotary Club of Makati- West Gift of Life, while also receiving support from advisers like Dr. Willie Ong. Today, Heart Warriors Philippines continues to grow as a nationwide network, committed to advocacy, medical missions, and providing hope to families facing the realities of heart disease.
