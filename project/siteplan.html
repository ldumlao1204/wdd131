Heart Warrior Philippines

Site Name:Hear Warrior Philippines

This name represents a non-profit organization on congenital heart disease awareness in the Philippines.

Optional Domain Availability: https://www.facebook.com/HeartWarriorsPhInc

Site Purpose

The site provides congenital heart disease awareness by providing medical information, both to parents and patients, along with a membership information form to each designated area. It also seeks donors and benefactors who will give financial and moral support to families living with congenital heart disease patients. It promotes love and hope within a community of people fighting with these diseases.
